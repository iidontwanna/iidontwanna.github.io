Claude Nouk is a dubbing mixer, sound designer and composer originally from NYC. In New York they curated as well as participated in music and sound art events for nearly 7 years before moving to Glasgow to focus on films and podcasts. They received a degree in Sound for the Moving Image with honours at Glasgow School of Art and have been living and working in the UK for 4 years. I perceive sound as elements that roam freely through every part of existence. If one can't hear it they see it and if they can't see, they feel it. I am always striving to give a story more life. Whether the sound is paired with a moving image or meant only to be heard, it must be captivating.
